# Go-To-Market Strategy

## Value Chain

```
You (freelancer) ──► SustainabilityAI Platform ──► TDi Sustainability ──► TDi's clients
                                                        │
                                      ┌─────────────────┼─────────────────┐
                                      ▼                 ▼                 ▼
                                 Bettercoal        EO100/ARC       Future clients
                               (coal industry)   (energy certs)   (mining, oil, etc.)
```

TDi Sustainability is our **first customer and channel**. They resell/white-label the platform to their own client portfolio.

---

## Why This Works

### TDi's Current Situation
- 5 separate digital tools (TEDD, Country Risk, Commodity Risk, ICAT, Search360)
- No AI capabilities in any of them
- Custom platform (platform-new.tdi-digital.com) that requires separate logins per tool
- Clients interact with a fragmented experience
- TDi doesn't have AI engineers — building this in-house is expensive

### What We Offer TDi
1. **One platform replaces 5 tools** — unified experience for their clients
2. **AI capabilities they can't build internally** — chatbot, automated reports, risk analysis
3. **White-label ready out of the box** — they rebrand for each client
4. **You already know their codebase** — you built it, you know where it breaks
5. **Faster client onboarding** — AI-guided setup instead of manual configuration

---

## Revenue Model

Option A: **Annual license fee** — TDi pays fixed annually, unlimited clients
Option B: **Per-client pricing** — TDi pays per branded instance
Option C: **Revenue share** — percentage of TDi's digital platform contracts

**Recommended**: Start with Option A (annual license). Clean, predictable, no usage tracking overhead. TDi wants to know their cost and resell at their margin.

---

## Demo Strategy

We don't need a full product to sell TDi. We need a compelling demo that shows:

1. **AI Chat Assistant** — "What do we need for EO100 Principle 3?" → answers from uploaded standards
2. **Multi-Framework Mapping** — enter data once, shows how it maps to EO100, MSCI, ISS
3. **Automated Report Generation** — assessment data → AI-generated executive summary
4. **White-Label Switching** — one click to change from "TDi" branding to "ARC Resources" branding

**Build order for demo:**
1. Knowledge base + chat (fastest, most impressive)
2. White-label theme engine
3. AI report generator
4. Framework mapping UI

That's the demo. TDi sees this and they understand immediately.
