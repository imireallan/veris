# GTM Strategy Based on Our Consultancy Client Full Service Catalog

## Customer's Business Model

Our Consultancy Client is not a product company — they're a **consultancy** that happens to have some digital tools. They sell 4 consulting service pillars (Risk Management, Risk Identification, Strategy, Impact & Leadership) across 11 industry sectors. The 4 digital tools are just delivery mechanisms for their consulting work.

## The Real Opportunity

They manually do what our AI platform can automate. When they do consulting for a mining company:
- Consultant manually maps OECD standards → our RAG knowledge base does this
- Consultant manually writes risk assessment reports → our AI generates them
- Consultant manually tracks supply chain issues → our Search360 replacement + AI alerts
- Consultant manually maps frameworks → our multi-framework engine does this automatically

## Our Value Prop to Our Consultancy Client

> "We provide the digital backbone for your ENTIRE consultancy — replacing 4 fragmented tools AND automating the manual work your consultants do today."

## 11-Sector Architecture

### Sector-Specific Configuration Engine

The platform must support sector-specific:
1. **Risk frameworks**: Automotive uses RBA, Mining uses TSM, Energy uses ESG Certification Standard
2. **Assessment templates**: Different due diligence requirements per sector
3. **Reporting standards**: Different disclosure frameworks per sector
4. **Stakeholder types**: Different engagement requirements per sector

4. **Reports MUST adapt to sector requirements**
